Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get an offer on my car?
Usually within 1–2 business hours. A real specialist reviews your info and texts you a number — no computer-generated quotes.
What is escrow and do I need it?
Escrow is a secure payment method where funds are held by a third party until the deal is complete. It's optional but highly recommended for buyer and seller protection. Learn more about our escrow services.
How do DMV services work?
Our licensed DMV specialist handles title transfers, registration, plates, lien releases, and all documentation. We streamline the entire process so you don't have to wait in line or deal with confusing paperwork. Learn more about DMV services.
Can I buy or sell a car from out of state?
Yes! We handle out-of-state transfers, including title work, registration, and compliance with both states' requirements.
How much does it cost to list my car?
Listing on E.V.Time is 100% free. We only take a small commission when your car sells.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. We never sell your data or share it with third parties. All transactions use secure, encrypted connections. Read our privacy policy.
What if I have an issue with a buyer or seller?
Contact us immediately at (424) 425-6211 or welcome.evtime@gmail.com. Our team will mediate and help resolve disputes fairly.
Do you offer financing?
Not directly, but we can connect you with trusted lending partners. Contact us for details.
Can I cancel a transaction?
It depends on the stage. Before funds are released via escrow, cancellations are typically possible. Contact us ASAP if you need to cancel.
