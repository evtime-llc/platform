Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get an offer?

Usually 1–2 business hours. We use real specialists, not automated quotes, so you get an honest evaluation.

What is escrow and do I need it?

Escrow is a secure third-party service that holds funds until both parties meet agreed terms. It's optional but highly recommended for safe transactions. Learn more about escrow →

Do you handle DMV paperwork?

Yes! We have a licensed DMV specialist on staff who handles title transfers, registration, plates, lien releases, and more. See DMV services →

Can I buy or sell a car from out of state?

Absolutely. We coordinate out-of-state transfers regularly and handle all multi-state documentation.

How much does it cost to list my car?

Listing is 100% free. We only take a small commission when your vehicle sells.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not sell personal information. All connections are encrypted. Read our privacy policy →

What if there's a dispute?

Contact us immediately at Support@itsEVTime.com or use this form. Our team will mediate and work toward a fair resolution.

Do you offer financing?

We don't provide financing directly, but we can connect you with trusted lending partners.

Can I cancel a transaction?

Yes, before funds are released from escrow. Contact us ASAP if you need to cancel.
