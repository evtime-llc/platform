What is Escrow?

Escrow is a secure payment method where funds are held by a trusted third party until all conditions of the sale are met. Think of it as a safety net for both buyer and seller.

Instead of sending money directly to a stranger, your payment is protected until you verify the vehicle, complete the inspection, and sign off on the deal.

Why Use Escrow?

  • Buyer Protection. Your funds are secured until you receive the vehicle and confirm it matches the listing.
  • Seller Protection. You're guaranteed payment once the buyer accepts the vehicle — no bounced checks or payment disputes.
  • Peace of Mind. No risk of fraud, scams, or misunderstandings. A neutral third party manages the entire process.
  • Professional Handling. We coordinate inspections, title transfer, and release of funds so you don't have to worry.

How It Works

1 Agree on Terms
Buyer and seller agree on price, vehicle condition, and inspection timeline.
2 Buyer Deposits Funds
Buyer securely transfers payment to the escrow account. Seller is notified funds are secured.
3 Vehicle Inspection
Buyer inspects the vehicle (in person or via trusted mechanic) to verify condition and authenticity.
4 Title & Documentation
Our DMV-licensed team verifies title, completes paperwork, and ensures legal transfer.
5 Funds Released
Once buyer approves and paperwork is complete, funds are released to the seller. Deal done.
