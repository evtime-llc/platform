Escrow Services

Safe transactions, every time.

EV Time coordinates secure vehicle transactions through licensed third-party escrow providers. Our structured process protects both buyers and sellers from fraud, payment disputes, and documentation errors — while keeping the experience smooth and professional.

Important Disclosure

Escrow services are administered by independent, licensed third-party escrow providers. EV Time is not a licensed escrow holder and does not hold buyer or seller funds. All funds are deposited, held, and released by the licensed escrow provider in accordance with agreed transaction terms.

What Is Escrow?

Escrow is a neutral, licensed third-party service that holds funds during a vehicle transaction. Money is released only after agreed-upon conditions — such as inspection, title verification, and documentation — are satisfied.

This structure protects both parties and creates a transparent, documented process from start to finish.

Why Use Escrow?

Buyer Protection

Funds are securely held by a licensed escrow provider while vehicle condition, title, and documentation are verified.

Seller Protection

Buyer funds are verified and secured before vehicle release. No bounced checks or payment reversals.

Documented Process

All terms, conditions, and timelines are clearly structured and recorded.

Professional Coordination

EV Time coordinates with licensed DMV partners, escrow providers, and both parties to ensure a clean handoff.

How It Works

1

Agree on Terms

Buyer and seller agree on price, vehicle condition, and timeline.

2

Funds Deposited

Buyer deposits funds with the licensed escrow provider.

3

Vehicle Inspection & Verification

Vehicle condition and documentation are reviewed according to agreed terms.

4

Title & Documentation

Licensed DMV partners process title transfer and required paperwork.

5

Funds Released

Once conditions are satisfied, the licensed escrow provider releases funds to the seller.
