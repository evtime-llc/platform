What is Escrow?

Escrow is a neutral third-party service that holds funds during a vehicle transaction. Money is only released when both buyer and seller have met all agreed-upon conditions.

Why Use Escrow?

Buyer Protection

Your funds are secure until you verify the vehicle condition, title, and all documentation.

Seller Protection

Know the buyer has funds available before releasing your vehicle. No bounced checks or payment disputes.

Peace of Mind

Both parties are protected by a transparent, documented process.

Professional Handling

Our team coordinates with DMV services, title transfers, and documentation to ensure a smooth handoff.

How It Works

1

Agree on Terms

Buyer and seller agree on price, vehicle condition, and timeline.

2

Buyer Deposits Funds

Buyer transfers funds to escrow account. Seller is notified.

3

Vehicle Inspection

Buyer inspects vehicle and verifies condition matches description.

4

Title & Documentation

Our DMV specialist processes title transfer, registration, and all paperwork.

5

Funds Released

Once buyer approves and title is transferred, funds are released to seller.
